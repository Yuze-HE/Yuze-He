Yuze He

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at AIoT Lab, Department of Information Engineering in The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I am supervised by Prof. Guoliang Xing. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Electrical Engineering, Zhejiang University (ZJU), China in 2019.

My research interests are in mobile sensing, mobile computing, and machine learning systems, with a current focus on the perception and intelligence of autonomous vehicles. My previous research has focused on developing cooperative systems that integrate autonomous vehicles with intelligent transportation infrastructures, with a vision of advancing autonomous driving with the support of smart infrastructures.
