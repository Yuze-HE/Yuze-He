Yuze He

I have recently obtained my Ph.D. degree at AIoT Lab, Department of Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). My Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Guoliang Xing. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, from Zhejiang University (ZJU), China in 2019.

My research interests lie at the intersection of mobile sensing, robotics, and machine learning systems. My previous research has focused on developing cooperative systems that integrate autonomous vehicles with intelligent transportation infrastructures, with a vision of advancing autonomous driving with the intelligence of edge/cloud. I have designed and implemented several new systems on 3D perception, localization, and HD mapping that have been tested in real-world scenarios. I received the MobiCom 2023 Best Community Contribution Award.
